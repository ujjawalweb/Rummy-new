Objective of Rummy

The primary objective of a rummy game is for players to have all their cards arranged in valid sequences and sets before they are able to declare and show their cards. Players when playing online rummy, aim to score the lowest points even if they are unable to arrange all their cards in groups of valid sequences and sets. However, winning is generally the top priority of all players and to do this they need to form at least one pure sequence accompanied by another sequence or set out of their rummy cards. All the other cards in a player’s hand can be arranged in any type of sequence or set so long as they are valid according to the rules of rummy. When one player declares and shows their hand of cards, all other players must also do so and depending on what cards each player has in their hand, they are assigned points based on the standard rummy points system.

How to Play Rummy on RummyBaazi

In online rummy, a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 6 players can join at the table. Each player is dealt a particular number of cards depending on whether you play 13 card rummy or 21 card rummy. The rummy card game is played with two or more decks of cards depending on the number of players. After the players have dealt their cards, the remaining undealt pile of cards is kept face down. This pile is known as the draw pile or the closed deck. A random card is selected from this pile of cards and this is marked as the cut joker, also known as the wild joker. There is another pile of cards that gradually builds up as the game of rummy proceeds. This is known as the open deck or discard pile.

The rummy card game is known by many as a draw and discard game. This is because when the rummy card game begins, each player has to first draw a card from either the open or the closed deck and then discard a card from their hand onto the discard pile. Simultaneously, players need to keep arranging their cards into valid sequences and sets. Finally, to win a game of online rummy, players must have all their cards arranged in valid sequences and sets, which will result in getting zero points.

Variants of Rummy Game

13 Card Rummy

The most popular variant, the 13 card rummy, has been given this name because each player is dealt 13 cards. It is considered the simplest and easiest form and that is why it is far more popular than any other variation of the rummy card game. Since this variation of rummy is played with just 13 cards, it is a swift and fast-paced rummy game that requires a high amount of concentration and flexibility in strategies. 13 card rummy is a truly exciting and adrenaline rushing experience for players. Moreover, the different types of gameplay modes in 13 card rummy make it highly challenging as well as a rewarding experience for players of all ages. Let’s look into the details of the different variations of 13 card rummy game.

21 Card Rummy

The 21 card rummy variation is by far the most complex form of rummy game. Unlike 13 card rummy, in this variant, players are required to form a minimum of 3 pure sequences, 8 dublees, 3 tunnelas, a group of 8 jokers, and 2 marriage sets. For 21 card rummy, there are certain specific rules and terms that you need to know in order to win this online rummy game.

Jokers have a special place in the 21 card variant since there can be multiple jokers in a single rummy game. The printed joker, the cut joker, the upper joker, and the lower joker, are all important rummy cards needed to successfully declare and show in a game of 21 card rummy. For example, if the 5 of Spades is randomly chosen as the cut joker, then the 4 of Spades and the 6 of Spades become the lower joker and the upper joker respectively for that particular rummy game.

Now, to win a game of 21 card rummy, players need to form dublees, tunnelas, and marriage hands/sets in addition to pure/impure sequences and sets. When playing 21 card rummy online, it is important to note that a dublee is a group of two cards of the same rank and suit, while a tunnela is a group of three cards of the same rank and suit.
